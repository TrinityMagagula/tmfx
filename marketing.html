Training & Mentorship

At TM Sales and Marketing, we're committed to developing the next generation of sales and marketing professionals through our comprehensive training and mentorship program. This paid program is designed to equip participants with the skills, knowledge, and experience needed to excel in sales and marketing careers.

Program Highlights:

  • Hands-on training in both direct and digital marketing techniques
  • Personalized mentorship from industry experts
  • Practical experience working with real client campaigns
  • Business ownership fundamentals for entrepreneurial paths
  • Portfolio development showcasing your achievements

Characteristics We Develop:

Our program focuses on cultivating these essential qualities of top-performing sales and marketing professionals:

  • Communication Excellence: Mastering both verbal and non-verbal communication
  • Product Knowledge: Deep understanding of products and value propositions
  • Persuasion Skills: Ethical influence and negotiation techniques
  • Resilience: Handling rejection and maintaining motivation
  • Adaptability: Adjusting approaches based on customer needs
  • Time Management: Prioritizing tasks for maximum productivity
  • Digital Proficiency: Leveraging technology for sales success
  • Emotional Intelligence: Reading and responding to customer emotions

Program Structure:

Our 12-week intensive program combines classroom learning with field experience:

  • Weeks 1-4: Core sales techniques and marketing fundamentals
  • Weeks 5-8: Specialization in chosen area (direct sales/digital marketing)
  • Weeks 9-12: Client project implementation with mentor supervision
  • Graduation: Certification and portfolio presentation

Participants receive a competitive stipend during the program with performance-based bonuses available.
