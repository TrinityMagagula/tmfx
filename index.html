About TM FX TRADING

Your trusted partner in forex trading education

At TM FX TRADING (Pty) Ltd, we are committed to empowering traders with comprehensive education and practical skills needed to navigate the dynamic forex markets. Our structured mentorship program is designed to take you from foundational concepts to advanced trading strategies, all while emphasizing risk management and disciplined execution.

Unlike traditional trading education, we provide hands-on experience through simulated demo trading environments before transitioning to live market conditions. This progressive approach ensures you develop confidence and competence at each stage of your trading journey.

Our team of experienced traders and educators brings years of market insight to every lesson, workshop, and coaching session. We don't just teach trading - we cultivate traders who can analyze markets, manage risk, and execute strategies with precision.

As we continue to grow, our mission remains clear: to deliver exceptional trading education that transforms beginners into confident traders and helps experienced traders refine their edge in the markets.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Do I need prior trading experience to join?

No prior experience is necessary. Our mentorship program is designed to accommodate all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced traders looking to refine their strategies.

What's the difference between demo and live trading?

Demo trading uses virtual funds in a simulated market environment, allowing you to practice without risk. Live trading involves real money and actual market conditions. We strongly recommend mastering demo trading before considering live accounts.

How much time should I dedicate to learning?

We recommend at least 5-10 hours per week for consistent progress. Our program is designed to be flexible to accommodate different schedules, but regular practice is essential for developing trading skills.

What trading platforms do you support?

Our education applies to all major trading platforms including MetaTrader 4/5, cTrader, and Deriv. We provide specific guidance for each platform during the mentorship program.

Can I participate if I'm outside South Africa?

Yes, our online programs are available to traders worldwide. All our materials are in English and our trading hours accommodate multiple time zones.
