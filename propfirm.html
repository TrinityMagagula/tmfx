Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our demo prop trading firm

Is this real trading with actual money?

No, AlphaSynth is a demo proprietary trading firm. All trading is conducted with virtual funds in a simulated environment. There is no real money involved in any aspect of our platform.

How do payouts work if it's all demo?

Our payouts are simulated by the company for educational purposes. When you reach profit targets, we simulate what a real payout would look like based on your performance, but these are not actual market profits.

What are synthetic indices?

Our synthetic indices are proprietary simulated markets that mimic the behavior of real financial instruments but are generated algorithmically. They allow for customizable volatility and market conditions, making them ideal for strategy testing.

Why should I pay for a demo account?

The fee covers access to our advanced platform, educational resources, and the structured evaluation process that helps traders develop discipline. Think of it as paying for a comprehensive trading education tool rather than for market access.

Can I upgrade my account after passing?

Yes, we offer scaling plans where successful traders can qualify for larger virtual accounts. This simulates how professional prop firms would increase capital allocation to proven traders.
