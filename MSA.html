master services agreement

This Agreement is entered into on [Date] by and between:

  1. TM FX TRADING (Pty) Ltd, Registration Number: [Insert Number], with its principal place of business at [Insert Address] ("Client"); and
  2. TM SALES AND MARKETING (Pty) Ltd, Registration Number: [Insert Number], with its principal place of business at [Insert Address] ("Agency").

1. Scope of Services

1.1 The Agency shall act as an independent contractor providing direct and online sales and marketing services for the Client’s forex-related products and services, including trading signals, mentorship programs, and other approved offerings.

1.2 Services include but are not limited to: lead generation, face-to-face sales, telesales, online sales campaigns, client onboarding, and brand promotion within South Africa.

1.3 Detailed responsibilities, KPIs, and deliverables shall be outlined in Annexure A.

2. Commission Structure

2.1 The Agency shall receive a commission of 25% of the gross amount of each successfully closed and confirmed transaction.

2.2 The Agency shall submit invoices monthly. The Client shall make payment within 15 days of receipt of a valid invoice.

2.3 Full commission terms and eligible campaigns are listed in Annexure B.

3. Independent Contractor

3.1 The Agency operates as an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed to create a joint venture, employment, or partnership relationship.

3.2 The Agency is solely responsible for its taxes, agents, operational costs, and compliance with applicable labour and business laws.

4. Agent Screening, Training & Conduct

4.1 The Agency shall ensure that all agents undergo identity verification, background checks, and training aligned with Client standards.

4.2 A detailed Code of Conduct and Compliance Checklist is attached in Annexure C.

4.3 The Client reserves the right to request removal of any agent violating the code of conduct or regulatory obligations.

5. Compliance Obligations

5.1 The Agency and its agents shall strictly comply with applicable laws including but not limited to:

  • FAIS Act (Financial Advisory and Intermediary Services Act, 2002)
  • POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013)
  • CPA (Consumer Protection Act, 2008)

5.2 The Agency shall not provide financial advice or guarantee investment returns.

5.3 Any breach shall be considered grounds for immediate termination.

5.4 The Agency acknowledges and agrees that it and its agents operate under an execution-only sales model. No financial advice, comparisons of financial products, or product recommendations shall be provided to customers. All product queries shall be referred to the Client’s authorised channels.

6. Confidentiality and Data Protection

6.1 All data, intellectual property, and customer information provided by the Client remain the exclusive property of the Client.

6.2 The Agency shall not share, store, or use personal data of customers outside the scope of this agreement and must ensure secure handling in accordance with POPIA.

7. Intellectual Property & Branding

7.1 The Client grants the Agency limited, revocable rights to use brand assets and materials strictly for campaign execution.

7.2 Unauthorized reproduction, alteration, or distribution of the Client’s content is strictly prohibited.

8. Audit and Monitoring

8.1 The Client reserves the right to audit the Agency’s operations, agent training records, and marketing practices with reasonable notice.

9. Indemnification

9.1 The Agency indemnifies the Client against claims resulting from misrepresentation, non-compliance, or misconduct by its agents.

9.2 The Client indemnifies the Agency against claims arising from defective products or services not under the Agency’s control.

10. Non-Solicitation and Exclusivity

10.1 The Agency shall not engage in direct competition with the Client or solicit its customers for alternative services during the term of this Agreement and for a period of 6 months post-termination.

10.2 Specific competitors may be listed in Annexure D if applicable.

11. Dispute Resolution

11.1 Disputes shall first be resolved through written negotiation. If unresolved, the matter will be submitted to mediation in [City]. If still unresolved, it shall be referred to binding arbitration under AFSA rules.

12. Termination

12.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice.

12.2 Breach of compliance, fraud, or reputational harm are grounds for immediate termination without notice.

13. Force Majeure

13.1 Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform obligations due to events beyond control (e.g., natural disasters, strikes, regulatory changes).

14. Entire Agreement

This Agreement, including all annexures, constitutes the full understanding between the parties and supersedes prior communications.

15. Limitation of Liability

15.1 Neither party shall be liable to the other for any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages, including but not limited to loss of profits, revenue, or data.

15.2 The total liability of either party under this Agreement shall not exceed the total amount of commissions paid to the Agency in the six (6) months preceding the claim.

16. Governing Law

16.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of South Africa. The parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the South African courts.

17. Regulatory Warranties

17.1 Each party warrants that it possesses and shall maintain all required licenses, authorisations, and approvals necessary to carry out its obligations under this Agreement, including but not limited to registration with the Financial Sector Conduct Authority (FSCA) if applicable.

17.2 The Agency warrants that its agents shall not offer financial advice unless duly authorised under the FAIS Act.

18. Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption

18.1 The Agency and its agents shall not, directly or indirectly, offer, give, solicit, or accept any bribe or other corrupt payment, inducement, or reward in connection with this Agreement.

18.2 Both parties agree to comply with all applicable anti-corruption and anti-bribery laws and to promptly report any breach of this clause.

19. Insurance

19.1 The Agency shall maintain appropriate levels of public liability and professional indemnity insurance throughout the duration of this Agreement and shall provide proof of such coverage upon request by the Client.

20. Temporary Contracting and Novation

20.1 In the event that TM SALES AND MARKETING (Pty) Ltd is not yet registered at the time of signing, the Client agrees to contract with the undersigned individual acting in their personal capacity as the intended sole shareholder of the Agency.

20.2 Upon registration of TM SALES AND MARKETING (Pty) Ltd, this Agreement shall automatically novate to the company without further action required, and all rights and obligations shall transfer accordingly.

Annexure A: Scope of Work, KPIs, and Deliverables

1. Services to be Rendered:

  • Lead generation (online and offline)
  • Face-to-face direct sales of approved products/services
  • Telesales and customer follow-up
  • Running online ad campaigns (Facebook, Instagram, Google)
  • Onboarding and assisting referred clients
  • Promoting the TM FX TRADING brand at events and online
  • Maintaining accurate referral records via the provided dashboard

2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

  • Minimum of 10 qualified leads per week per active agent
  • Minimum 3 closed sales per week per agent (for paid subscriptions or mentorship)
  • 95% compliance with onboarding and data handling procedures
  • 100% attendance at required training and compliance refreshers
  • 24-hour response time to client or prospect queries
  • Zero instances of giving unlicensed financial advice

3. Deliverables:

  • Weekly report of activities and lead/sale outcomes
  • Monthly sales summaries and invoice submissions
  • Attendance records of all agents at compliance training
  • Access to a clean agent roster and identification documents
Annexure B: Commission Terms and Eligible Campaigns

1. Commission Breakdown:

  • 25% of gross value from successful client subscription or mentorship sign-up referred by Agency
  • Commission applies only to the first confirmed payment (e.g., first month's fee, full mentorship payment)
  • Recurring commissions apply only if explicitly approved in writing

2. Payment Terms:

  • Valid invoices submitted by the 5th of each month
  • Payment due by the 20th of the same month
  • Bank details must match registered business name of the Agency

3. Approved Campaigns:

  • TM FX TRADING Monthly Signal Subscription
  • TM FX Mentorship Bootcamp
  • Referral Sales via TM FX Web Platform (tracked)
  • Online Courses officially listed by TM FX

4. Exclusions:

  • Sales to family members of agents (without approval)
  • Self-referrals
  • Free or trial-based sign-ups
Annexure C: Code of Conduct and Compliance Checklist

Agent Conduct Rules:

  • Agents must always identify themselves truthfully as representatives of TM SALES AND MARKETING operating on behalf of TM FX TRADING.
  • Agents must not promise guaranteed profits or investment returns.
  • Agents must never pressure or mislead prospects.
  • Agents must wear official ID badges (physical or digital) when operating in public.

Compliance Checklist:

  • Verified identity documents for all agents
  • Signed agent declaration of understanding on FAIS/POPIA/CPA
  • Completed training on TM FX TRADING services and prohibited practices
  • Completed training on execution-only sales model and prohibition of financial advice under the FAIS Act
  • Adherence to data privacy practices when collecting client information
  • Ongoing internal audits every 3 months

Disciplinary Process:

  • First breach: Written warning
  • Second breach: Suspension pending investigation
  • Third breach or severe violation: Removal from project and possible legal action
Annexure D: Restricted Competitors and Exclusivity Terms

Restricted Competitors:

The Agency may not represent or engage in sales for any of the following companies (or their subsidiaries) during the term of this Agreement and for 6 months thereafter:

  • FX Goat (Pty) Ltd
  • Pip Hunters SA
  • Learn to Trade (SA)
  • Global Forex Institute
  • Any other local or international forex education/trading signal provider operating in South Africa, unless approved in writing

Exclusivity Notes:

  • Agency agrees not to distribute or market competing forex signal or mentorship services under any personal or third-party brand during the term.
  • All TM FX TRADING marketing and materials must not be repurposed or reused for other brands, even post-contract.
