How It Works:

Each participant receives a demo account with Synth Markets containing 1000 ZAR. The competition runs weekly, and winners are determined by:

  • Being in the top 100 traders with an account balance exceeding 3000 ZAR by week's end
  • Having their trading activity reviewed for compliance with competition rules

If your account balance falls below the minimum, you can top up your demo account with another 1000 ZAR for a 50 ZAR fee in the cashier section.
